About

Daniel Martins
do Carmo

Former professional footballer turned licensed sports agent — bringing a player's instinct to every negotiation.

Born in Rio de Janeiro, Daniel played at Fluminense and gained international experience before transitioning to agent work. In January 2022, he orchestrated the transfer of Samir Caetano to Watford FC in England — a landmark deal that cemented his place in the industry.

Today, Daniel leads Inside Football and co-manages Rivorelli Sports, one of Rio de Janeiro's most respected agencies. His philosophy is simple: a player's growth on the field must be matched by their development as a human being.

Our Services

Contract Negotiation

Full advisory support in contract negotiations with national and international clubs — always prioritising athlete welfare, transparency, and fair outcomes for all parties.
