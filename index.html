ABOUT DANIEL MARTINS

My name is Daniel Martins do Carmo, born in Rio de Janeiro. Passionate about football, I began my journey as a professional athlete, with stints at Fluminense and international experiences. This time on the field gave me a broad perspective of the sport, which made my transition to the backstage of football much smoother. After retiring as a player, I started working as a scout and currently operate as a football agent, focusing on the complete career management of each athlete. In January 2022, I took part in my first major negotiation — transferring my athlete Samir to Watford Football Club in England. This was a major milestone and marked my full immersion into this industry. Even though I’m young, I constantly strive to learn, improve, and evolve. Today, I’m the founder of a career management company and also a partner at Rivorelli Sports, a well-known sports agency in Rio de Janeiro. My goal goes beyond the sporting aspect — I aim to help athletes grow both as professionals and as human beings. I believe in relationships built on trust, mutual learning, and the construction of a solid future for everyone involved. After all, in football — as in life — we are always learning and reinventing ourselves.

OUR SERVICES

Contract Negotiation

We provide complete advisory support in negotiating contracts with national and international clubs, always prioritizing, above all, the well-being of the athlete and their family. We value transparency, ethics, and responsibility throughout every stage of the process, seeking to balance the interests of the club and the agent. Our goal is to achieve a fair and beneficial agreement for all parties involved.

Career Management

Strategic planning of the athlete’s career, identifying opportunities and setting short, medium, and long-term goals. The athlete is required to be studying and maintaining good grades as an essential part of their development both on and off the field. In addition, an English course will be recommended so that the athlete can continue their education and expand future opportunities, ensuring they never stop improving academically.

International Transfers

With expertise in international transfers, we operate with a global network of contacts and deep knowledge of regulatory standards. In every negotiation, we seek reliable local partners in the destination countries, ensuring each move is handled with total focus on the athlete’s well-being.

Legal Advisory

Specialized legal support in sports law, with all negotiations carried out in full compliance with regulations. The company has a highly qualified and trustworthy legal team dedicated to protecting the athlete’s interests at every stage.
